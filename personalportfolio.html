Why I Am Taking This Course

For many years I have searched for a career path that sparked a passion and a feeling that told me I could do it forever. Or at least, for a couple dozen years of my life. I tried out cooking, management, nutrition, accounting, and finance. Then, as I was starting to feel comfortable in my work with quality assurance, software engineering popped up on my social media feed. As I lingered on the contents of each post, the app's algorithm began to match my interest, and coding tips and tricks filled my screen. It aligned with my passion for creation and efficiency, my natural talent in mathematics, and my desire to continue working for a tech company that would support my work-life balance.
